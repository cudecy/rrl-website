Our Services

Our Core Services

Middle Income Real Estate Development

We build lovely, decent contemporary homes for middle income earners which help affordability, provides amenities that are required for this sect of buyers. Our focus is to provide homes close enough to major central business areas that can be purchased by middle income earners; using tactical project structures that suit their purchase patterns. We focus on providing solutions to Company Staff Groups, Staff Cooperatives, Business Groups etc.

Middle Income Real Estate Sales

We work with other developers to sell units en-bloc (not on retail basis) to match their products strategically to target middle-income earner buyer groups.

Millennial & New Generation Real Estate Advocacy & Training

Rock Realty provides advisory sessions to staff of Corporate Organisations, Staff Cooperative Groups and other youth groups on how to purchase real estate and home ownership. We seek to gradually begin to re-orientate African youths towards commencing real estate investments early in life and building their real estate portfolio as they build their career.

Real Estate Advisory

We offer real estate advisory to organisations on how to occupy, finance and manage their property in order to reduce costs, maximise productivity, and future-proof their estate.
