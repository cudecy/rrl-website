CEO-MD History

Ibukun Adebayo is an astute finance & real estate professional with extensive experience in various capacities in the finance & real estate industries, with over 10 years’ experience in investment banking, development finance and real estate transaction advisory services. She established Rock Realty Limited, a Real Estate Development & Advisory Company where she currently serves as the Managing Director/Chief Executive Officer. She has won several awards for her innovative inventions in middle income real estate. She also owns shares in & sits on the board of companies which include Cornucopia Consulting Limited, an Investment Consulting firm, Today’s Bukka; an African-themed foods restaurant that provides excellent African cuisine and BlueFruit Limited that specializes in Oil and Gas servicing. She is the author of the best seller book “I Want my Home”, An easy guide to Home Ownership for Middle Income earners. Ibukun is the convener of the Women in Real Estate Africa (WIRE Africa) network; which serves as a platform that empowers, educates and provides collaboration opportunities for women in the real estate sector across Africa. She is passionate about wealth building through real estate investing. She published several articles on Funds Management and Private Wealth Management in the Company Publications and was responsible for providing content for the periodic Funds Newsletter for ChapelHill Denhman Management Limited. Subsequently she took a role as the head of real estate at The Infrastructure Bank PLC., - a Development Finance Institution - where she led major deals in infrastructure developments in the country. She worked with private & corporate investors & other DFIs to structure viable financing packages for greenfield & brownfield projects. She is also a financial modelling expert and has led financial modeling teams & anchored several modelling training sessions in the various organizations where she served. Ibukun earned her first degree in Medicine & Surgery, and subsequently took several courses in investment banking. She is currently undertaking a masters in real estate in the New York University.
